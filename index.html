About AB Music Platform

Vision & approach

AB Music Platform is an independent music platform created by Aleksandr Bakanov to publish original releases under multiple artist projects.

The focus is on strong lyrics, coherent storytelling, and a responsible approach to copyright. Every track goes through a complete cycle — from concept and writing to distribution on digital platforms with correct metadata.

The platform includes several genres: cinematic ballads, deep house, seasonal music, and instrumental projects. The core priority remains artistic authenticity and quality control.
